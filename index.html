    Breastfeeding Technical Support

    Successful breastfeeding depends on the positioning and attachment of your baby to your breast. When your baby is positioned and attached correctly, they will feed well and you will feel no pain. As you start breastfeeding, you may feel some discomfort but you will get used to the feeling. There are a number of different breastfeeding positions, so find one that suits you and your baby. We are professional in provideing the required technical assistance for correct position attachment.
